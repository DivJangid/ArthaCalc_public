Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-02-01

Divyanshu J built ArthaCalc as a commercial app. This page informs visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information.

Information We Collect

  • Usage Analytics

How We Use Your Data

We collect minimal information to improve your experience:

Analytics Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This includes:

Screen views and feature usage
App performance metrics
Device type and operating system version

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Local Storage

Your app preferences (theme, language, number format) are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
Financial data from your calculations
Location data
Contacts or other personal files

All calculations are performed locally on your device. Your financial data never leaves your phone.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for analytics purposes. These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information. We only use services that respect user privacy and comply with applicable data protection laws.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Since we do not collect personal or financial information, there is minimal risk to your privacy. Your app preferences are stored securely on your device using standard mobile storage mechanisms.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also object to data processing, request data portability, or withdraw consent where applicable. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed as a general-purpose financial calculator and does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
